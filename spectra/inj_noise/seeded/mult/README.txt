modified 'comb' lensing template maps by multiplying by a scalar number
'p' indicates multiplication of a number greater than 1 (and 'n' less than 1); integer indicates the percentage increase/decrease
for example: p3 would mean a multiplication of the lensing template map by 1.03x, n10 would mean .90x, etc

example 'comb' block for p3:
	if comb:
        	m = 1.03 * m


'doubled_LT' simply refers to scalar multiplication of the lensing template map by 2 (m = 2 * m)
